Services

Therapy for the things that quietly pile up, and the moments that change everything. If you are not sure where to start, we can decide together in a quick, low pressure chat.

Anxiety and stress

Quieting the noise of anxiety and stress, so you can breathe, think, and move through your day with more ease.

Steadier days, better sleep, clearer decisions.

Depression and low mood

Gently rebuilding energy, motivation, and connection when things feel heavy or flat for too long.

More movement, more meaning, more self respect.

Trauma and grief

Making room for what happened and what you lost, at a pace that feels safe and steady.

Care that respects your story and your timing.

Relationships and family

Clearer communication, healthier boundaries, and less repeating the same patterns in relationships.

More honesty, more repair, more calm at home.

Life stages and change

Support through the seasons of change: identity shifts, parenting, aging, and big decisions.

Stay grounded while life is moving.

Mind body support

Practical regulation tools that support your nervous system, your body, and your day to day stability.

Approaches

I tailor the work to your needs and goals, using approaches that support real change while keeping sessions human and grounded.

1

Cognitive and values based support

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy to reduce overwhelm and move toward what matters.

2

Relational and family focused work

Family systems, family and marital therapy, and person centered support for clearer connection and boundaries.

3

Regulation and compassion

Breathwork, compassion focused, and supportive therapy for steadiness, self trust, and emotional safety.
